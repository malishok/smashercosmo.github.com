Карьера

The last eight years I was dealing with the design of Web sites. My role in this relationship changed over time. Initially, I was responsible for a small frgamenty located on pages: banners, icons, and illustrations.

Then I began to trust the design of key pages. As a rule these were the main ones. But time passed and the pages that needed to issue it became more. And then something happened that turned my understanding of Web design - began with an increase in Number of change and quality objectives.

Функциональная сложность сайтов стала возрастать. В мои обязанности вошло построение форм, и разработка новых контролов. После чего я влюбился в функциональный дизайн. И понеслась.
